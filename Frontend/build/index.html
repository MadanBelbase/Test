Search OSM Statistics

How it works

  1. Enter a hashtag used in OSM changesets.
  2. Select date range or use quick-select (D/W/M).
  3. Choose a topic to filter specific features.
  4. Click Get Statistics for instant data.

Scope of Data

OSMSG tracks all contributions made to OpenStreetMap through the standard API. It analyzes labels, comments, and hashtags to give you a deep-dive into community activity.
